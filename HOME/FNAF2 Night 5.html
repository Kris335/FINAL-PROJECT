Gameplay

Night 5 is the fifth playable night of Five Nights at Freddy's 2, as well as being the final night of the main storyline. This is one of the hardest nights in the game, as every animatronic, besides Golden Freddy, is incredibly active. The difficulty level is at a point where quick reflexes are essential to the completion of the night. On this night, the player needs to be even more careful, as the toy animatronics will most likely visit the Office more frequently.
