Gameplay

Unlike the previous nights, there is only one animatronic active on this night, being Nightmare Fredbear. The breathing the other Nightmare animatronics make is replaced by laughter. Upon completing this night, the player will unlock the Extra menu, with the Animatronics and Making Foxy features available in said menu.
