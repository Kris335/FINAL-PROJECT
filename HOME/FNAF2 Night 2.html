Gameplay

This night is the last playable night in the regular PC Demo version of the game. This night is much more difficult than the previous night, as Toy Freddy, Toy Chica, and Toy Bonnie are more active. The Music Box also unwinds faster. This is also the night where Balloon Boy, Mangle, and Withered Foxy become active, with the rare possibility of Withered Freddy, Withered Bonnie, and Withered Chica also making an appearance, forcing the player to adapt their play style in order to accommodate for these new additions.
